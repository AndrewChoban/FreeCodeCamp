Lost Ark

About the game

Lost ark is a free-to-play massively multiplayer online action role-playing game (MMOARPG). It was fully released in South Korea on December 4, 2019. The game was a;sp released in Europe and Americaas on February 11, 2022.

Classes

There are five main classes to choose from, then you can choose a specialization for that class for a total of fourteen different play styles. Some classes have more or less specializations than others.

Some of the classes are gender locked. The game does allow you to test your specialization for your class before making a final decision. Howeve, you must create a new character for each class first.

Warrior
Warriors are the powerhouses of Arkesia. They may not be the most nimble, so they stand their ground and make up for mobility with cataclysmic impact.

Martial Artist
These quick-footed fighters like to rush the enemy with combined attacks at lethal speeds that can only be described as breakneck.

Gunner
Whether wielding high-tech weapons or bows and arrows that never jam, keep an eagle eye on these sharpshooters or they may even hit you with their worst shot.

Mage
These mystical conjurers tap into the ancient magic of Arkesia to cast deadly spells on evil forces, heal the wounded, or support allies.

Assassin
These mysterious melee-makers fight demonic with demonic, channeling the dark powers in the name of the light in quick, colorful attacks.

My thoughts

Overall the game is fine, but it is nothing groundbreaking. The combat is fun but simple and by no means challenging during the main storyline. Now the story I cannot really speak about as I have been skipping the dialoge and cutscenes as much as I can. I am more interested in getting to end game content and seeing if I truely enjoy the game. I cannot say I would reccommend the game unless you are just looking for something new to play.
